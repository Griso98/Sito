Who am i?

My name is Alessio Grisanti, i'm from Treviglio (near to Bergamo and Milan), and my greatest passions are drawing and travel. For this reason, i've decided to base my website on an hypothetical travel agency based in Milan. I've studied at the "Liceo artistico Simone Weil", where i've had the opportunity to experiment with numerous artistic technics (such as oil painting, watercolor painting, traditional drawing, digital drawing, sculpiting and animation). In this period i'm trying not to use the pencils and and to draw directly with the pen. In the future i would like to work as an artist in the game or movie industry, so i'm trying to learn 3d modeling and rendering software, such as zBrush, Maya and Fusion 360.
